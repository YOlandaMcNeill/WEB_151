How To Play?...


1. Download the free app from your app store and sign in with your Facebook or email account or create one.

2. Choose from different playing options. Play against friends, strangers, the computer or yourself!

3. Start by making a word from the letters you were given making sure the first letter block is on the "star" square. Alternating turns, create words horizontally and vertically from your blocks and the ones on the board. Earn points by adding each letter's value together and by placing blocks on certain squares on the board. Earn coins for other prizes by winning games.
